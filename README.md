## MARS

Mars is a computer, that is not driven by you performing clicks but you sharing your your intention and the OS handles the rest.

The apps don’t live in screens, but are tools for the os. You want to listen songs, your computer plays them without you opening an app, choosing to click play, searching something. You say “play Kabira.” It does.

You don’t open excel and draft a quotation, you say “email the quotation to Mr. Neil.” Your computer finds what Mr. Neil needs, shows you what it created, and its sent.

You don’t draft a professional message to your boss anymore, you just say “Tell my boss I won’t be coming tomorrow because I am sick.” His computer receives and tells him, no need to generate bullshit from AI for it to be summarised by AI anyways.

You wanna cook something, you don’t find the recipe, or find the correct video, you say “I wanna make poached egg.” the system shows you the video. You get stuck, it helps.
